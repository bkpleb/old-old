Timeline

Here is a small timeline of my "tech journey" in a way.

  • 2015: I got a Lego Mindstorms EV3 (home edition) that year and it introduced me to programming.
  • 2016 - 2018: Did various EV3 related projects at my school's robotics program.
  • 2020: Attended my first infosec conference focused on dronsec. Did my first ctf.
  • 2020 - 2021: Competitive programming frenzy period. Was extremely active on Kattis, didn't give a damn of Codeforces & Leetcode. Did olympiads during this timeframe.
  • 2021 - present: Contributing to Mozilla as an NDA'd SUMO contributor, KB writer, and Social Support Moderator.
  • 2022 - present: Casually do ctfs, learn infosec, don't let computer bugs bite.
  • 2023: Got started with the lightning network and node experiments. As well as learning Solidity to get started with smart contract ctfs, etc.
    • As you can see, I live an extremely normal life. No prodigy hacker at the age of 17, no big brains, and no smart-ass results. Everyone has different circumstances and situations, just make best use of your own.
